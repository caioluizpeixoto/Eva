# **App Name**: EVA Template Pro

## Core Features:

- Sales Letter Structure: Mimic the original landing page sales letter structure.
- Template Display: Display EVA templates, organized by categories such as flowers, letters, animals, and more.
- Offer Highlighting: Clearly showcase the limited-time offer and irresistible pricing (e.g., R$10.00), emphasizing urgency and scarcity.
- Subscription tier: Ability to subscribe for a monthly or yearly period.
- Pop-up notifications: Notifications of recent purchases

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A vibrant red (#F44336) to draw attention to urgent offers and prices.
- Background color: Off-white (#FCF8FB) to provide a clean, focused backdrop.
- Accent color: Teal (#009688) for call-to-action buttons, offering a cool contrast to the red.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans' sans-serif for legibility and a modern feel. Note: currently only Google Fonts are supported.
- Use clean and simple icons related to template categories and benefits.
- Subtle animations for notifications to mimic social proof and purchase alerts.
